ABOUT US

Tiny Horse is an award-winning marketing and production company that emerged as a response to the digital transformation happening in the media industry and paved the way in sustaining and monetizing digital audiences around premium content on any platform.

Our background in production and expertise in marketing allows us to tell our clients’ stories in engaging, coordinated and efficient ways across the digital, linear, and physical ecosystems. Today, our reach extends far beyond the world of entertainment to include Fortune 50 clients, studios, and start-ups.
